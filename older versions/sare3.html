The Problem

The Agricultural Payment
System Is Broken

Three critical pain points across the agricultural value chain. One solution: Bitcoin, made accessible through Batolink-powered smart cards.

🏪
Merchants & Agro-Dealers
Can't Accept Digital Payments
  • Rural merchants locked out of digital payment infrastructure
  • Card networks charge 3-5% fees — unsustainable for agriculture
  • No access to cross-border customers or diaspora buyers
  • Cash handling risk, theft, no transaction records
  • Cannot build credit history without digital transaction data
👩‍🌾
Farmers & Producers
No Store of Value
  • Paid in cash — no savings mechanism, no security
  • Local currency loses value; no access to inflation hedges
  • Cannot receive payments from international buyers directly
  • No verifiable income history for loan applications
  • Excluded from the global digital economy entirely
🌎
Diaspora & Institutional Buyers
Friction at Every Step
  • 7-12% remittance fees to send money home for goods
  • 3-7 day settlement for cross-border agricultural trade
  • No way to purchase goods directly for rural delivery
  • Multiple currency conversions eat into margins
  • No payment-linked traceability for supply chains
